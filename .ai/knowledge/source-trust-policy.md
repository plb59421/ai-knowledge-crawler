# Source Trust Policy

[中文](#中文) | [English](#english)

---

## 中文

本文定义信息源可信度分级，用于排序解释、人工复核和冲突处理。

### S 级

原始研究、官方论文、研究机构发布。

- ArXiv
- OpenAI
- DeepMind
- Anthropic
- OpenAlex

### A 级

高质量技术博客、模型社区、研究社区。

- Hugging Face Blog
- Hugging Face Daily Papers
- The Gradient
- BAAI Hub

### B 级

新闻聚合、中文技术媒体和行业资讯。

- 机器之心
- 量子位
- Google AI Blog
- Meta AI Blog

### 使用规则

- 同一主题出现冲突时，优先参考 S 级来源。
- 新闻媒体内容应尽量回链到原始论文、官方公告或模型仓库。
- 排序可以使用来源可信度作为加分项，但不能替代内容质量评分。

## English

This policy defines source trust tiers for ranking explanations, human review, and conflict handling.

### Tier S

Original research, official papers, and institutional releases.

- ArXiv
- OpenAI
- DeepMind
- Anthropic
- OpenAlex

### Tier A

High-quality technical blogs, model communities, and research communities.

- Hugging Face Blog
- Hugging Face Daily Papers
- The Gradient
- BAAI Hub

### Tier B

News aggregators, Chinese technical media, and industry updates.

- Jiqizhixin
- QbitAI
- Google AI Blog
- Meta AI Blog

### Usage Rules

- Prefer Tier S sources when multiple sources conflict on the same topic.
- Media articles should link back to original papers, official announcements, or model repositories whenever possible.
- Source trust may contribute to ranking, but it must not replace content quality scoring.
