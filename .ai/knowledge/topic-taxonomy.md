## 中文

本分类用于知识库归档、标签映射和报告筛选。

### 核心主题

- `llm`: 大语言模型、基础模型、语言理解与生成。
- `multimodal`: 图像、视频、音频、视觉语言模型。
- `agent`: 工具调用、工作流、自动化智能体。
- `alignment`: 对齐、安全、红队、可靠性。
- `reasoning`: 推理、数学、规划、代码能力。
- `robotics`: 机器人、具身智能、控制。
- `infrastructure`: 推理服务、训练系统、芯片、部署。
- `science`: 科学发现、生命科学、材料、物理。
- `evaluation`: Benchmark、评测、基准数据集。
- `application`: 医疗、金融、教育、企业服务等落地方向。

### 评分参考

- 8-10：重大模型、框架、论文或产业事件。
- 5-7：有明确技术价值或工程参考价值。
- 1-4：普通资讯、重复新闻或低技术密度内容。

## English

This taxonomy is used for knowledge base archiving, tag mapping, and report filtering.

### Core Topics

- `llm`: Large language models, foundation models, language understanding and generation.
- `multimodal`: Image, video, audio, and vision-language models.
- `agent`: Tool use, workflows, and autonomous agents.
- `alignment`: Alignment, safety, red teaming, and reliability.
- `reasoning`: Reasoning, math, planning, and coding capabilities.
- `robotics`: Robotics, embodied intelligence, and control.
- `infrastructure`: Inference services, training systems, chips, and deployment.
- `science`: Scientific discovery, life science, materials, and physics.
- `evaluation`: Benchmarks, evaluations, and benchmark datasets.
- `application`: Healthcare, finance, education, enterprise services, and other applied domains.

### Scoring Guidance

- 8-10: Major model, framework, paper, or industry event.
- 5-7: Clear technical value or engineering reference value.
- 1-4: General news, repeated coverage, or low technical density.
